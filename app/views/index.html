A 'Live Tube Map' made with AngularJS, Google maps and Powered by TfL Open Data. To put simply, Tube data is requested, analysed, coordinates calculated, then marked on a Google map. When the data updates, the markers animate!

Looking for a developer with an eye for design? Have any questions? Say hello@tubemapp.co.uk :]

I made this primarily to learn AngularJS and work with Live Data. I live in London so a live tube map seemed ideal... and useful. What you'll see on the map is as accurate as the data coming in, which at times, is erratic. There is plenty of $scope for development and improvement but for the moment, enjoy! © 2015
