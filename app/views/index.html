A 'Live Tube Map' made with AngularJS, Google maps and Powered by TfL Open Data. To put simply, Tube data is requested, analysed, coordinates calculated, then marked on a Google map. Every 50s the data updates and the markers animate!

Looking for a developer with an eye for design? Have any questions? Say hello@tubemapp.co.uk :]

What you'll see on the map is as accurate as the data coming in, which at times, is erratic. Some coordinates are perfect, others, a best guess. You may notice anomalies, especially around the Liverpool street area where strange things occur! This is bad data! Anyway, there is plenty of $scope for development and improvement but for the moment, enjoy! © 2015
